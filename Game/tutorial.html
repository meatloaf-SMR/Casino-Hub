Game Tutorials

Blackjack

  • Your goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Face cards = 10, Aces = 1 or 11.
  • Click Deal to start, then choose Hit or Stand.
  • Dealer must hit until 17.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2.

Pool

  • Move your mouse to aim the cue ball.
  • Hold left mouse button to build power.
  • Release to shoot.
  • Sinking balls increases your score.
  • If you sink the cue ball, it resets to the starting spot.

Roulette

  • Choose a chip value, then click the betting layout to place bets.
  • Inside bets pay more but are riskier.
  • Outside bets (red/black, odd/even) pay 1:1.
  • Click Spin to start the wheel.
  • If the ball lands on your number or category, you win.

Poker (5‑Card Draw)

  • Click Deal to receive 5 cards.
  • Click cards to “Hold” the ones you want to keep.
  • Click Draw to replace the rest.
  • Your final hand is compared to the dealer’s.
  • Winning hands include pairs, straights, flushes, full houses, etc.
